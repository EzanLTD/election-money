About Election Money

Interested in money in Illinois politics? This site makes availablebulk downloads of Illinois campaign contributions and expenditures from the Illinois State Board of Elections going back to 1994.

Campaign Disclosure in Illinois

The following are campaign disclosure forms required under the Illinois Campaign Disclosure Act:

Campaign Disclosure Form Description
D-1 Form (i.e. Statement of Organization) A form providing basic committee information, registering a political committee with the Illinois State Board of Elections. This form is filed after a political committee has exceeded $5000 in either receipts or expenditures over a year (the filing threshold), at which point they are covered by the Illinois Campaign Disclosure law and thus are required to report contributions and expenditures accordingly.
D-1-A Form A supplement to the D-1 Form, only required if the political committee indicated is a legislative caucus committee established by 5+ members in the same Senate caucus or 10+ members in the same House of Representatives caucus.
D-2 Form (i.e. Report of Campaign Contributions and Expenditures) A form for filing a quarterly report, a final report, or an amendment to a report. These reports include summary totals of a political committee's contributions, expenditures, debts, fund balances, and investments over a given reporting period.
Schedule A A form for reporting all itemized contributions to a political committee over $150. These itemized contributions are reported quarterly.
Schedule A-1 A form for reporting a contribution of $1000 or more from a single source.
Schedule B A form for reporting all itemized expenditures from a political committee over $150. These itemized expenditures are reported quarterly.
Schedule B-1 A form for reporting an independent? expenditure of $1000 or more.
Schedule B-9 A form for reporting all itemized independent expenditures from a political committee over $150
Schedule C A form for reporting all itemized outstanding debts and obligations of a political committee over $150
Schedule I A form for reporting all itemized in-kind contributions to a political committee over $150.

Team and credits

This is a DataMade project done in collaboration with the Illinois Campaign for Political Reform that was spawned at a meeting held in Chicago with the Sunlight Foundation. We built this site to increase the access to campaign contribution data for journalists, activists and curious citizens.

Code

All the code for this site is open source. It consists of a scraper and this website to display the data. It was built with:

  • Flask - python web framework
  • Amazon S3 - file storage
  • Bootstrap - HTML and CSS scaffolding
  • Datatables - searchable, sortable HTML tables
  • Heroku - website hosting

All the code for this project is available on Github:

  • election-money - this website
  • campaign-finance_state_IL - Illinois State Board of Elections website scraper
